Panhandle Humane Society

The Mission of the Panhandle Humane Society is to prevent neglect, abuse and cruelty, as well as the overpopulation of pets and homeless animals.

Our goal is accomplished by educating the public and pet owners in the humane treatment of animals, increasing pet adoptions, and maintaining an animal shelter which is dedicated to providing humane treatment and care of all animals.

We are not associated with the Humane Society of the US and do not receive any funding from them. All of our donations come from our loyal supporters.
